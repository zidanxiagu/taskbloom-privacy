Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2026

TaskBloom ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

Data Stored Locally:

  • Task information (titles, dates, times, notes)
  • User preferences and settings
  • Subscription status

All your task data is stored locally on your device. We do not upload your personal task data to any server.

2. Voice Recognition

When you use voice input to create tasks:

  • Voice data is processed by Apple's Speech Recognition service (on iOS) or Google's Speech Recognition service (on Android)
  • We do not store or transmit your voice recordings
  • Voice recognition is processed according to Apple's or Google's privacy policies

3. In-App Purchases

Subscription and purchase transactions are processed by:

  • Apple App Store (for iOS)
  • Google Play Store (for Android)

We do not collect or store your payment information. All transactions are handled securely by Apple or Google.

4. Data Security

Your data is stored locally on your device and protected by your device's security features. We recommend:

  • Using device passcode or biometric authentication
  • Keeping your device software up to date
  • Regular backups through iCloud or Google Drive

5. Third-Party Services

Our app may use the following third-party services:

  • Apple/Google Speech Recognition for voice input
  • Apple App Store / Google Play for purchases

6. Children's Privacy

Our app is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your data (stored locally on your device)
  • Delete your data by uninstalling the app
  • Export your data through device backup
