Summary: TaskBloom respects your privacy. All your task data is stored locally on your device. We do not collect, transmit, or share any personal information.

1. Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal information.

TaskBloom stores all data locally on your device, including:

  • Tasks and reminders you create
  • Task templates and recurring task settings
  • App preferences and settings

2. Data Storage

All your data is stored exclusively on your device using local storage. Your data is:

  • Never uploaded to external servers
  • Never shared with third parties
  • Completely under your control

3. Voice Recognition

TaskBloom uses your device's built-in speech recognition feature to convert voice input into text. Voice processing is handled by your device's operating system (iOS/Android) according to their respective privacy policies. TaskBloom does not record, store, or transmit your voice data.

4. Third-Party Services

TaskBloom does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

5. Children's Privacy

TaskBloom does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13 years of age. The app is designed for general audiences and stores all data locally.

6. Data Security

Since all data is stored locally on your device, the security of your data depends on your device's security settings. We recommend using device passcode/biometric protection.

7. Your Rights

You have complete control over your data:

  • Access: View all your data within the app
  • Delete: Delete individual tasks or clear all data
  • Export: Your data remains on your device

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
